Our Mission Statement

Our goal is to work as a cohesive unit to build and program our robot to the best of our abilities.

We want to bring out the best in each other, to challenge each other, and to be active members of the Space Lions Team. We hope to bring together our unique talents and skill sets in the robotics world to create a successful robot. We also hope to create bonds with one another as well as our sister FTC teams, the 4326 Basement Lions and the 9681 Parallel Lions.

The Space Lions wish to go through the season representing our school well and following the core values of our school:

Life of the Mind

Mature Behavior

Mutual Respect

A Secure and Healthful Environment

A Balance Between Individual Achievement and a Caring Community
